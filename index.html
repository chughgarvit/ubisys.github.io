About Us

The UbiSys Research Group, led by Dr. Suchetana Chakraborty, explores core areas of ubiquitous computing, activity recognition, health sensing, and low-power embedded systems. Our team combines expertise across sensing, signal processing, systems, and HCI to solve real-world challenges.

Open Positions

  • BTP / MTP Students: Apply through the internal CSE department portal. Slots announced every semester.
  • PhD Applicants: Apply via the IIT Jodhpur PhD Admissions. Mention your interest in UbiSys in your SOP.
  • Postdoctoral Fellows: Email the PI with your CV and a short proposal.
  • Collaborators / Interns: If you’re from another institute, reach out with your portfolio and interests.
  • Alternatively, you can fill the below application form if the above methods do not work.

Application Form

How to Reach Out?

Send an email to suchetana@iitj.ac.in with your CV, interest area, and how you'd like to contribute to the lab.

Formal applications must be submitted through official IIT Jodhpur portals.
